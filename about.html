Hi, I'm Rachel

and I'm currently pursuing a degree in Communication Design at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh with a minor in Human Computer Interaction.

I'm interested in bringing clarity and understanding to complex systems to allow individuals to navigate and react accordingly to their current realities. To design is to craft new narratives and propose preferable futures.

I'm also interested in speculative critical design, social justice, and technology. Outside of design, I'm rowing on the Allegheny, casually Dogspotting, or cleaning up a failed kitchen experiment. Feel free to ask me about my work or about the time I got a boat stuck in a tree.
