I recently graduated from Carnegie Mellon University studying Communication Design with a minor in Human Computer Interaction, and am currently seeking opportunites in the SF Bay Area.

Much of my work concerns itself with the invisible and creating understanding. I'm interested in visualizing and clarifying abstract systems to allow individuals to navigate and react accordingly to their current realities.

I'm also interested in technology, speculative critical design, and social justice. If I'm not designing, I'm probably rowing, thinking about dogs, or cleaning up a failed kitchen experiment. Talk to me about my work or about the time I got a boat stuck in a tree.
