i'm currently pursuing a degree in communication design at carnegie mellon uiversity with a minor in human computer interaction. i'm also working on brand, experience, and digital product development at symkala. i value recognizing and acknowledging the social implications and potential of design, figuring out what it means to design, and finding new spaces where design can intervene.


i'm also interested in speculative and critical design, social justice, and “new aesthetic.” outside of design, i'm rowing on the allegheny river or casually dogspoting. feel free to ask me about my work or the time i got a boat stuck in a tree.
