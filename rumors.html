A visual narrative detangling fact and fiction

This book translates the various voices and timelines of the story from season of Serial, which examines the disappearance and murder of Hae Min Lee and the conviction of her exâ€“boyfriend Adnan Syed, into a digestable form.

There are various stories and narratives represented in the book: the accounts from Adnan, court proceedings, classmates's recollections of the day Hae's disappeared, anecdotes from community members. Each voice claims to be the authority; it becomes difficult to seperate fact from fiction.

Each type of information is treated differently; anecdotes and rumors from those who knew Adnan, the editorialization from the series's narrator, Adnan's recollections in court, the prosecution's key witness are organized and seperated so that the viewer can come to their own conclusion.
