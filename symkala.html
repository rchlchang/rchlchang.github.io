product, brand, and experience design for symkala.

symkala is a data synthesis and analysis software that provides subject-matter experts a smooth workflow to easily import, archive, analyze, and visualize different kinds of information including photos, social media, statistical, and GIS data. photos, witness accounts, and other human intelligence provide nuanced views of a region on the ground, while geopatial data tell the story from above. symkala combines the two and fills a gap in knowledge.

user studies

guided by the experience of humanitarian, intelligence, and finance experts, i designed symkala for non technical users in mind, creating an intuitive workflow and interface.

prototypes symkala is composed of four steps: importing and archiving of data, creating organized stacks and rearranging data, visualizing data, and exporting said visualizations.
